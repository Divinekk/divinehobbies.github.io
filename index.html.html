What is Afrobeat Dancing?

Afrobeat dancing is a high-energy style of dance that originated in West Africa and is now popular all over the world. It combines traditional African dance movements with modern urban influences, resulting in a dynamic and exciting dance style that is both fun and challenging.

This is a section on LEGWORK

LEGWORK is a Nigerian dance that involves action of the legs moving left and right.




This is a section on AZONTO

AZONTO is a Ghanaian dance that involves knee bending and hip movements.




This is a section on GWARAGWARA

GWARAGWARA is a South African dance that involves lifting and swinging one leg, then pulling the rest of your body into the movement.
